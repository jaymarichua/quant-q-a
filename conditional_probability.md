<h5>Conditional Probability</h5>

Quick mathematics questions in probability mainly involve Bayes' theorem and Expected Value (EV).

Bayes' theorem (2nd form)

$P_E(H) = P(H)P_H(E) / [P(H)P_H(E) + P(~H)P_{~H}(E)]$

Expected Utility

EU(A)=∑o∈O PA(o)U(o)
